Skardu


Skardu, is located in the Gilgit Baltistan region of northern pakistan is a picturesque town known for its natural beauty and unique culture. Skardu is known as a gateway to some of the world's highest peaks, including the renowned K2, attracting adventure enthusiasts. Skardu presents stunning landscapes, that include lush meadows and turquoise lakes making it a paradise for nature lovers. Visitors can indulge in various outland activities such as mountaineering, hiking and climbing etc making a must see destination for anyone visiting skardu.

Hunza Valley


Hunza Valley is situated in the gilgit baltistan region of northern pakistan. It is known for its cultural richness and beauty, surrounded by the karakoram range. Hunza valley is a popular tourist attraction for its famous historic forts known as altit and baltit forts and the rakaposhi mountain making it a perfect destination for anyone who loves nature and history.

Fairy Meadows


Fairy Medows is the heart of breathtaking himalayas in pakistan, surrounded by majestic peaks and lush greenery. Fairy Meadows is famous for its stunning picturesque views of the legendary 'Nanga Parbat' known as the 'Killer Mountain' making it a perfect destination for adventure and nature lovers. The meadows is surrounded with vibrant wildflowers offering a exhilarating setting fo hikers and campers. Fairy Medows is a place allowing visitors to reconnect with the simplicity and wonders of the great outdoors.

Attabad Lake


Attabad lake is located in the hunza valley of northern pakistan is a mesmerizing turquoise lake that attract visitors with its stunning beauty. Attabad lake has become a popular destination for travelers seeking respite in the lap of natural beauty. In attabad visitors can indulge themselves in various outland activities such as serene boating, adventurous treks along with surrounding hills. The lake provides surrent backdrop for nature and adventure enthusiasts to explore the beauty of the region.

Ratti Gali Lake


Ratti Gali lake is a beautiful hidden spot located in Neelum Valley, Azad Kashmir, Pakistan surrounded by majestic himalayas. The lake enchants visitors with serene blue waters reflecting the surrounding snow-capped mountains and greenery offering a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of the city life. Ratti gali lake provides an ideal setting for nature and adventure enthusiasts to immerse themselves in the breathtaking beauty of the kashmir landscape.

Neelum Valley


Neelum Valley is situated in the enchanting Azad Kashmir region of Pakistan, is like a piece of paradise on earth. It is surrounded by himalayas. The valley is the breathtaking sight with its waterfalls, lush green forests and windy rivers that attract visitors. Further, Neelum valley offers a peaceful serene retreat for visitors.
