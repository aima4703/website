Outland Activities





Camping

Experience the thrill of camping with outland adventurists in Northern Pakistan Regions by spending your nights under the starry skies with a stunning view. Immerse yourself in the peacefulness of lush green medows and vibrant wildflowers, making your camping experience in northern pakistan region truly unforgettable.
Season Summer and early autumn, from may to september
Locations Fairy Meadows, Ratti Gali Lake, Shogran Valley and Neelum Valley each showcasing unique landscapes and views.
Duration Offers one-night stays and multi-day journeys for a personalized camping experience.
Necessary Equipment Provided tents, sleeping bags, camping stoves, flashlight, insect repellent and other equipments for a seamless experience.
Safety Tips Comprehensive safety sessions covering instruction on setting up your camp, wildlife awareness and environmental care.

Horse Riding

Horse Riding can be a great way to improve physial strength, balance and co-ordination while offering a unique connection with a nature. Discover the enchantment of horse riding amid the stunning and most captivating landscapes of northern pakistan.
Season May to September
Locations Deosai plains, kaghan Valley, and Fairy Medows
Necessary Equipment Proper Riding attire, including appropriate footwear and helments is important for safe and enjoyable horse riding experience.
Safety Tips Follow instructions provided by our experience guides.

Rafting

If you are looking for a thrilling outland adventure then rafting could be the perfect choice. Feel the excitemnet as you navigate the thrilling rapids of the northern pakistan regions famous river that are surrounded by the breathtaking beauty of the karakoram and Himalayan mountain ranges.
Season Spring to Summer months from May to August
Locations Hunza Valley, Gilgit Rivers, Indus River and Swat River.
Necessary Equipment We provide safety equipment that include life jackets, helmets and paddles, ensuring a safe rafting adventure
Safety Tips Wear appropriate safety gear such as helmets amd life jacket also carefully follow guide instructions.

Boating

Embrace the beauty of northern pakistan's breathtaking waterways as you embark on our exhilarating boating adventures by exploring the crystal-clear lakes of saif-ul-muluk and attabad lake.
Season Spring and Summer season, from april to september
Locations Attabad lake, Saif-ul-muluk lake, Satpara lae and Shangrila lake
Necessary Equipment For a safe and secure boating experience for all our visitors, we will be providing life jackets, boat safety kits and Oars.
Safety Tips Wear a life jackets at all times while on the water and follow boating regulations and local rules.

Ice Skating

Glide through a frozen landscapes with exhilarating ice skating activity that enchances balance, coordination and muscle strength. Explore the frozen lakes in Gilgit Baltistan and pristine valleys in Chitral that offers an enchanting way to embrace the winter season while surrounded by the breathtaking beauty of Northern Pakistan.
Season Winter season, from December to February
Locations Attabad lake, Satpara lake and shangrila resort
Skating Sessions Our expert guide will provide informative skating sessions such as open skate, and different other lessons catering to skaters for all levels and age groups.
Necessary Equipment To ensure comfortable and enjoyable experience for visitors, we will be providing them rental skates, safety gear such as helmets and padding and helpful skating aids.

Cycling

Experience the excitement of cycling adventure and discover the enchanting landscapes of Northern Pakistan. Ride the cycle through the breathtaking scenic routes and enjoy the thrill of exploration and charm of Northern Pakistan as our cycling tours are designed for individuals who want to challenge themselves and explore the untamed beauty of the region.
Season Even though our cycling tours are available throughout the year, but the best months for cycling is from May to November when the weather is pleasant.
Cycling Routes Popular cycling routes in gilgit baltistan regions include Deosai Plains, khanabad, karakoram highway and Skardu valley.
Necessary Equipments We provide all the necessary equipments, including high-quality cycles, helmets and safety gear ensuring a secure cycling experience.
